# Customer Guide

## üõí Placing an Order

### Step 1: Browse Menu
1. Visit the restaurant's ordering website
2. Browse categories (Burgers, Pizza, Drinks, etc.)
3. Use search to find specific items
4. View item details by clicking on item cards

### Step 2: Add to Cart
1. Click on menu item
2. **Customize your item:**
   - Select size (Small, Medium, Large)
   - Add extra toppings
   - Remove ingredients you don't want
   - Add special instructions
3. Adjust quantity
4. Click "Add to Cart" ($XX.XX)

**Cart Tips:**
- Cart saves automatically
- Edit items by clicking on them in cart
- Remove items with trash icon
- Cart persists even if you close browser

### Step 3: Choose Order Type

**Pickup**
- Pick up from restaurant location
- Usually ready in 15-30 minutes
- No delivery fees
- Show order number when picking up

**Delivery**
- Enter delivery address
- System checks if you're in delivery zone
- Delivery fee shown before checkout
- Track driver in real-time (if restaurant-delivered)

### Step 4: Select Timing

Depending on restaurant's order model:

**ASAP (Immediate)**
- Quickest available time
- Usually 15-30 minutes
- Best for when you're hungry now

**Schedule for Later**
- Choose specific date and time
- Must be within restaurant's advance window (usually 7 days)
- Perfect for party orders or planned meals
- Restaurant confirms availability

### Step 5: Checkout

1. **Review Order Summary**
   - Items and customizations
   - Subtotal
   - Delivery fee (if applicable)
   - Tax
   - Total

2. **Enter Contact Information**
   - Name
   - Phone number
   - Email (for confirmation)

3. **Delivery Address** (if delivery)
   - Street address
   - City, state, postal code
   - Delivery instructions (apartment #, gate code)
   - Validate address is in delivery zone

4. **Add Tip** (optional)
   - 10%, 15%, 20%, or custom amount
   - Goes directly to restaurant/driver

5. **Payment**
   - Credit/debit card via Stripe
   - Secure payment processing
   - Save card for faster checkout

6. **Place Order**
   - Review everything one last time
   - Click "Place Order"
   - Receive confirmation email immediately

## üìÖ Scheduling Orders

### When to Use Scheduled Orders
- Catering for events
- Party orders
- Busy restaurant prep times
- Ensure availability of specific items

### How to Schedule

1. After adding items to cart, click **"Schedule for later"**

2. **Choose Date**
   - Calendar shows available dates
   - Grayed-out dates are unavailable
   - Restaurant's closed days are hidden

3. **Choose Time**
   - Select from available time slots
   - Based on restaurant's operating hours
   - Accounts for prep time needed

4. **Advance Notice**
   - Restaurant may require minimum notice
   - Large orders may need more lead time
   - System will show earliest available time

5. **Confirmation**
   - Scheduled orders require restaurant acceptance
   - You'll receive confirmation within set time
   - If rejected, you'll be notified with reason

### Managing Scheduled Orders

**View Upcoming Orders**
- Go to "My Orders"
- Filter by "Scheduled"
- See all future orders

**Modify Scheduled Order**
- Must modify at least X hours before scheduled time
- Cancel and reorder if needed
- Contact restaurant directly for urgent changes

**Reminders**
- Email reminder 24 hours before
- WhatsApp reminder 1 hour before (if opted in)
- Push notification on day of

## üöö Delivery Options

### Restaurant-Managed Delivery
Restaurant uses their own drivers.

**Pros:**
- Integrated tracking
- Direct communication with driver
- Reliable service

**Process:**
1. Order placed
2. Restaurant prepares food
3. Driver assigned
4. Track on map in real-time
5. Driver delivers to door

### Customer-Arranged Delivery
Order pickup, arrange your own delivery (Uber, DoorDash, etc.)

**Pros:**
- Sometimes faster
- More delivery options
- Restaurant focuses on food quality

**Process:**
1. Select "Pickup" as order type
2. Place order
3. Order separate Uber/DoorDash
4. Give them restaurant address and order number
5. They pick up and deliver to you

**Tips:**
- Verify order is ready before driver arrives
- Include order number in delivery notes
- Tip your delivery driver appropriately

### Delivery Zones

**In Zone:**
- Address validated automatically
- Standard delivery fee applies
- Estimated delivery time shown

**Outside Zone:**
- System notifies you during checkout
- Can still order for pickup
- Or use customer-arranged delivery

**Zone-Based Pricing:**
- Closer zones may have lower fees
- Further zones may have higher minimums
- Check delivery fee at checkout

## üìç Tracking Your Order

### Order Status Updates

**Pending**
- Order just placed
- Awaiting restaurant confirmation
- Usually confirmed within 1-2 minutes

**Confirmed**
- Restaurant accepted order
- Starting preparation
- ETA displayed

**Preparing**
- Food is being made
- Kitchen actively working on order
- Can't cancel at this stage

**Ready for Pickup**
- Food is ready
- Head to restaurant with order number
- Check-in at counter

**Out for Delivery**
- Driver assigned (if restaurant delivery)
- Track on map
- See estimated arrival time

**Completed**
- Order delivered/picked up
- Leave a rating/review
- Save receipt for records

### Real-Time Updates

**Email:**
- Confirmation immediately
- Status change notifications
- Receipt after completion

**WhatsApp** (if you opt in):
- Real-time status updates
- Rich messages with photos
- Direct reply to restaurant

**In-App:**
- Track on order page
- Live status bar
- Countdown timer to ready time

### How to Track

1. **Via Email Link**
   - Click tracking link in confirmation email
   - No login required
   - Bookmark for easy access

2. **Via Order Number**
   - Go to restaurant website
   - Click "Track Order"
   - Enter order number and phone number

3. **Via Account** (if logged in)
   - Go to "My Orders"
   - Click on active order
   - View live tracking

## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Can I modify my order after placing?**
- ASAP orders: Contact restaurant immediately by phone
- Scheduled orders: Modify up to X hours before scheduled time
- After preparation starts: Usually can't modify

**What if I'm running late for pickup?**
- Food stays warm for reasonable time
- Contact restaurant if significantly delayed
- May need to reheat if very late

**What if my delivery is late?**
- Check tracking for real-time status
- Contact restaurant via chat or phone
- Delays sometimes happen during busy periods

**Can I reorder a previous order?**
- Yes! Go to "My Orders"
- Click "Reorder" on past order
- Review and checkout

**How do I save my favorite items?**
- Create an account
- Click heart icon on menu items
- Access from "My Favorites"

**What if I have allergies?**
- Check item descriptions
- Use special instructions
- Call restaurant for serious allergies
- Restaurant cannot guarantee 100% allergen-free
