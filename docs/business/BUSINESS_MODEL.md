# QSR Ordering Platform - Business Model

## Overview
Our platform follows a **100% software-only BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) model** with a generous free tier and optional premium features. Zero hardware costs, zero setup fees, zero commission.

---

## üéØ Business Philosophy

### Core Principles
1. **BYOD First** - Use devices you already own (tablets, phones, computers)
2. **Software Only** - No expensive proprietary hardware to buy
3. **Free to Start** - Full-featured free tier with unlimited orders
4. **Scale with Growth** - Add premium features only when you need them
5. **Fair Pricing** - Aligned with restaurant success, not order volume
6. **No Lock-in** - Own your data, export anytime, cancel anytime

### BYOD Philosophy
- **Kitchen Display:** Use any tablet/iPad you already have
- **POS Integration:** Works with Square, Stripe Terminal, or any payment processor
- **Printing:** Connect any ESC/POS thermal printer via USB/network
- **Tablets:** iOS, Android, or web browser - your choice
- **No Vendor Lock-in:** Standard protocols, not proprietary hardware

### Target Markets
- **Phase 1 (Now)**: Independent restaurants (1-3 locations) - BYOD model
- **Phase 2 (6 months)**: Small chains (4-10 locations) - Advanced features
- **Phase 3 (12 months)**: Franchises & brands (10+ locations) - Enterprise features
- **Future**: Hardware bundles available but never required
